Our Target Audience

Our target audience is businesses, residents, and visitors of the Point of the Mountain area.

Businesses: We provide resources and support to help businesses grow and succeed.

Residents: We offer information and events that help residents connect with their community.

Visitors: We provide information and resources to help visitors make the most of their time in the Point of the Mountain area.
